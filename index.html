Product Range

We are one of the foremost names engaged in manufacturing and exporting a variety of Automatic Pouch Packing Machines (Form Fill and Seal Machines) that is available in latest Mechanical-PLC Operated designs for various ranges from 1 gram to 10000 grams.

Powders
With our advancements in tech and engineering, we've developed machines for non free flowing powders like Chilli, Turmeric, Corriander, Wheat Flour[Atta], Maida, Corn Flour, Chemical powders, etc with high accuracy & speed in pouch packing. We provide the following:

  • Double Servo Augur Fillers

  • Single Servo Augur Filler

  • Pneumatic/Volume metric Cup Fillers

Granules
We have developed machines for free flowing Granules like Dal, Sugar, Ravva and raw spices with high speed in pouch packing. We provide the following:

  • Volume metric with adjustable Cup Filler

  • Cup Filler in collar & shoot system

  • Weigh Filler in Collar & Shoot system

Liquids
We've developed liquid packing machines for edible oils, auto-mobile oils, ghee and semi-liquid products. We provide the following:

  • Servo System with high speed

  • Semi-liquid like Ghee,Paste,Batter

  • 3 side or 4 side for laminated pouch packing
